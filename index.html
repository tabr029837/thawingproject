Build To is the total amount for the day. Usage Sold is the actual demand. Daily Pull follows your corrected formula, which simplifies to the same amount as `Usage Sold`, and that daily pull is what the analyzer tracks through the 48-hour thaw and 96-hour expiration window.

Enter your data in the highlighted columns. The `Usage Sold` column is set up for quick tabbing straight down.
